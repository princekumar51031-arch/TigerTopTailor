About Us

Umesh Prajapati
25 Years Experience

Tiger Top Tailor is a premier tailoring shop dedicated to crafting perfect garments for every customer. Our journey started in 2001 with a small offline shop, serving local clients with love and precision. We specialize in custom stitching for men, women, and kids, combining style with comfort. Each garment is tailored to perfection, ensuring quality fabric handling and accurate measurements. Our team provides expert guidance on fabrics, designs, and patterns to bring your ideas to life. We also offer sewing tools, training sessions, and repair services to support your sewing journey. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of our operations, with personalized attention for every order. Timely delivery and professional finishing are our promises to each client. With 25 years of experience, Umesh Prajapati leads the team to maintain high standards consistently. We continue to innovate, learn, and adapt to modern trends while honoring traditional tailoring techniques.
